ABOUT US

About Spike Pest Control Pvt. Ltd.

We are Spike Pest Control Pvt. Ltd., a leading provider of professional pest control services in India. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, we specialize in offering pest control solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Our expertise lies in providing safe and effective pest control treatments to address a wide range of pest infestations. Whether it's eliminating rodents, termites, bed bugs, or other pests, we ensure thorough and reliable service to protect your property.

In addition to our extensive experience in pest control services, we have over 7 years of experience in manufacturing quality pest control products. This enables us to offer innovative solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you maintain a pest-free environment.
